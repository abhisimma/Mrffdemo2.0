MR Future Foundation

enhancing lives, sustaining futures

MR Future Foundation is a registered non-profit organization under the Indian Trust Act, accredited with 80G, 12A, Darpan, and CSR Form 1 certifications, reflecting its strong commitment to transparency, compliance, and social responsibility. Dedicated to enhancing the quality of life, the Foundation focuses on development initiatives that align with the 5Ps of sustainable development: People, Planet, Prosperity, Partnership, and Peace. The Foundationâ€™s efforts are guided by the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), NITI Aayog's strategic framework, CSR provisions, and Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013, ensuring impactful and sustainable change across its areas of focus.

CSR Form 1

This registration empowers us to engage in corporate social responsibility initiatives, enabling partnerships with businesses to create impactful projects that align with our objectives.

80G Certificate

This certification provides tax exemptions for donations made to MR Future Foundation, encouraging both individuals and corporate entities to support our initiatives.

12A Registration

This registration allows us to receive tax-exempt donations, facilitating increased financial support for our projects and initiatives.

Darpan Registration

As part of NITI Aayog's initiative, this registration recognizes our trust and enhances our connection to government schemes and programs aimed at social development.

E Anudhan Registration

This certification enables us to access various government funding schemes, enhancing our capacity to implement projects aligned with national development goals.

Our Commitment

These certifications underscore our dedication to ethical practices, enabling us to maximize our impact on the communities we serve. At MR Future Foundation, we prioritize transparency and accountability in all our operations, ensuring that contributions are utilized effectively for the greater good.
