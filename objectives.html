Our Objectives

MR Future Foundation was established with a vision to serve society by addressing key social, economic, and environmental challenges through sustainable development initiatives. As a public charitable trust, our mission is driven by a strong commitment to improving the lives of disadvantaged communities. We align our objectives with the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) defined by the United Nations and the NITI Aayog policy framework of India.

1. End Poverty in All its Forms Everywhere

  • Provide financial support, vocational training, and employment opportunities.
  • Promote social safety nets and access to basic services.

2. End Hunger and Promote Sustainable Agriculture

  • Support agricultural initiatives for food security.
  • Promote sustainable farming practices.

3. Ensure Healthy Lives and Well-being

  • Enhancing accessibility to quality healthcare services for all individuals.
  • Develop and execute comprehensive health education programs for communities.

4. Ensure Quality Education

  • Improve access to education for all levels.
  • Promote lifelong learning and skills development.

5. Achieve Gender Equality

  • Provide equal opportunities to empower women and girls everywhere.
  • Strive to eliminate all forms of discrimination and violence against women worldwide.

6. Clean Water and Sanitation

  • Provide safe and affordable drinking water and sanitation.
  • Promote water conservation practices.

7. Affordable and Clean Energy

  • Promote the adoption and expansion of sustainable and renewable energy sources.
  • Advance widespread adoption of innovative energy efficiency programs globally.

8. Decent Work and Economic Growth

  • Support small businesses and innovation.
  • Promote job creation and decent work conditions.

9. Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

  • Promote and implement effective energy efficiency initiatives on all levels.
  • Drive sustainable industrialization and promote innovation for global advancement.

10. Reduce Inequality

  • Promote inclusion and reduce economic disparities.
  • Advocate for equal treatment for all.

11. Sustainable Cities and Communities

  • Enhance living conditions in urban and rural areas through sustainable initiatives.
  • Enhance disaster resilience.

12. Responsible Consumption and Production

  • Reduce waste and environmental impact.
  • Encourage sustainable lifestyles.

13. Climate Action

  • Mitigate and adapt to climate change.
  • Promote awareness of climate change and encourage sustainability worldwide.

14. Life Below Water

  • Protect marine ecosystems and reduce pollution.
  • Support sustainable use of ocean resources.

15. Life on Land

  • Restore and conserve terrestrial ecosystems.
  • Work to combat desertification and prevent the ongoing loss of biodiversity globally.

16. Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

  • Promote good governance and rule of law.
  • Reduce violence, corruption, and injustice.

17. Partnerships for the Goals

  • Foster collaboration and mobilize strong partnerships with key stakeholders.
  • Promote global cooperation for sustainable development.
